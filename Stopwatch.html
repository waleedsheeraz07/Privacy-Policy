Privacy Policy

Stopwatch  •  Effective Date: 09/02/2026

Stopwatch is a simple timing application that allows users to start, pause, reset, and measure elapsed time with precision. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Stopwatch does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or non-personal data.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not require user registration and does not request any personal information. All timing operations are performed locally on the device and are not saved or transmitted.

  • No data collection of any kind
  • No storage of timing history
  • No background data processing
  • No ads, analytics, or tracking technologies

Offline Functionality

Stopwatch operates entirely offline. An internet connection is not required, and the app does not perform any network communication.

Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, advertising platforms, or analytics tools.

Children’s Privacy

Stopwatch is suitable for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, it complies with children’s privacy protection regulations.

Data Security

Because no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of unauthorized access, data leakage, or misuse.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
