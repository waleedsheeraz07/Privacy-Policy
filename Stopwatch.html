Privacy Policy

Stopwatch App  •  Effective Date: 19/02/2026

Stopwatch is a simple and precise timing application designed to measure elapsed time with start, pause, and reset controls. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Stopwatch does not collect, store, share, track, or transmit any personal or non-personal data — ever.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not require registration, accounts, or permissions. Timing data exists only temporarily on the screen and is discarded immediately when reset or when the app is closed.

  • No personal information collected
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No background processes
  • No ads or behavioral profiling

Offline Functionality

Stopwatch operates entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection and performs no network communication of any kind.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising services, or external APIs.

Children’s Privacy

Stopwatch is safe for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, the app complies fully with children’s privacy protection regulations.

Data Security

As no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data leakage, misuse, or unauthorized access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any revisions will be published on this page.
