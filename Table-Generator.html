Privacy Policy for Table Generator

Effective Date:

Table Generator is a simple Android application that allows users to generate multiplication tables (from 1 to 10) by entering a number. Your privacy is respected and protected.

Information Collection and Use

Table Generator does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal information.

  • No user data is requested
  • No data is stored locally or remotely
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No ads, analytics, or tracking tools are used

The number entered by the user is processed only to generate the multiplication table and is not saved or retained in any form.

Offline Usage

Table Generator works entirely offline. An internet connection is not required, and the app does not perform any network communication.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising platforms that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Table Generator is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, the app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Because no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data misuse, leakage, or unauthorized access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
