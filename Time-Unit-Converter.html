Privacy Policy

Time Unit Converter  •  Effective Date: 13/02/2026

Time Unit Converter is a simple application that allows users to enter a value in days, hours, minutes, or seconds. The app automatically converts the value into the other time units instantly.

Time Unit Converter does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or non-personal data.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not require user registration and does not request any personal information. All calculations are performed locally on the device and are not saved or transmitted.

  • No data collection of any kind
  • No local or remote storage
  • No background data processing
  • No ads, analytics, or tracking technologies

Offline Functionality

Time Unit Converter operates entirely offline. An internet connection is not required, and the app does not perform any network communication.

Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, advertising platforms, or analytics tools.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, it complies with children’s privacy protection regulations.

Data Security

Because no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access.
