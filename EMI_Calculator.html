Privacy Policy

EMI Calculator  •  Effective Date: 09/02/2026

EMI Calculator is a simple financial utility that allows users to calculate monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment), total payment, and total interest by entering:

  • Loan Amount
  • Annual Interest Rate (%)
  • Loan Tenure (in months)
This application operates entirely offline and does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

Information Collection and Use

EMI Calculator does not require user registration, account creation, or personal details. All calculations are performed locally on the device and remain visible only on the user’s screen.

  • No personal data is collected
  • No financial information is stored
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No internet connection is required

Offline Functionality

The app functions completely offline. It does not connect to external servers, APIs, or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

EMI Calculator does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics providers, or third-party SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

Since no information is collected from any users, EMI Calculator complies with children’s data protection regulations.

Data Security

As the app does not collect or store any data, there is no risk of unauthorized access, data misuse, or data leakage.
